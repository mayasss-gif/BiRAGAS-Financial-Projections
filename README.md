## What's covered

### Markets sized (27)
- **Foundational** — AI Drug Discovery · RNA-Seq R&D · RNA Therapeutics · Target ID & Validation · Companion Diagnostics
- **Oncology** — Total + NSCLC, Breast, Colorectal, Prostate, Melanoma, Pancreatic, Hepatocellular
- **AML** — FLT3-ITD resistance · WDR4 driver · biomarker stratification
- **Kidney** — Diabetic Nephropathy (26.2% CAGR) · CKD-non-diabetic
- **Immune / Autoimmune** — IBD · RA · SLE · MS · Psoriasis/PsA · CTD-other
- **Neurodegeneration** — Alzheimer's · Parkinson's · ALS
- **Metabolic / Other** — Obesity · Depression / TRD

### Revenue streams (7)
1. Big Pharma platform license — $1.80M / customer / yr
2. Mid-cap biotech modular license — $0.70M
3. Small biotech SaaS — $0.20M
4. CRO white-label license — $1.00M
5. AMC research-tier license — $0.15M
6. Government / NIH contract — $0.25M
7. Co-development partnerships + Companion Diagnostic royalties
